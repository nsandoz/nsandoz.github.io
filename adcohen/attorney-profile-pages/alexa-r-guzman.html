Background and Experience

While attending Loyola Law School, Ms. Guzman served as an extern at the U.S. District Court for the Central District for the Honorable Andrew J. Guilford. During her externship Ms. Guzman assisted in researching and editing judicial opinions. She was also involved with the Loyola Center for Conflict Resolution where she mediated divorces, neighbor disputes, landlord/tenant disputes, and creditor/debtor disputes. During her time at Loyola, Ms. Guzman was inducted into Alpha Sigma Nu and was a member of La Raza and St. Thomas More Law Honor Society.

Ms. Guzman joined the Law Offices of Adrienne D. Cohen as a law clerk in September 2019 and became an Associate in November 2019. Her practice includes insurance defense.
