Passion. Experience. Diligence.

The Law Offices of Adrienne D. Cohen is an energetic and dynamic group of lawyers with a wide variety of experience both in and out of the courtroom. We focus on insurance defense and coverage, including personal injury, construction defect litigation, and appeals, and personal injury. We aggressively and professionally represent our clients’ interests. Our common-sense approach to litigation has proven to be a successful formula which our clients appreciate and our opponents respect.

Ms. Cohen, the founder and principal, has represented major insurance companies and their insureds for more than 30 years. She has earned a membership in the American Board of Trial Advocates (ABOTA) through her extensive jury trial experience. During that time, she has actively participated in both public and private community affairs, including volunteering for youth groups, teaching, and serving on various Boards of local organizations.

For more detailed information on Ms. Cohen’s background and experience, as well as the background and experience of the firm’s other attorneys, please visit our Attorney Profiles page.
