About

Nicky's Place is a contemporary american-style restaurant featuring hand-crafted sandwiches & burgers, fresh salads, homemade soup, fresh fish, braised short ribs and chicken entrees in a beach-casual atmosphere. The interior was designed to have the feel of a summer vacation house. Inspired by coastal homes everywhere, the warm interior, vintage beach photos, ceiling fans along with delicious food and refreshing drinks, makes the Nicky's Place the perfect place to enjoy the vacation lifestyle year round! If you're looking for delicious, hand-crafted eats look no further — Nicky's Place will make you feel right at home!
