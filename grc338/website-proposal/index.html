Introduction

Roast

Roast is a small, one location coffee shop that brings your delicious fair-trade coffee roasts, friendly service, and scrumptious pastries at an unbeatable price.

Target audience

The purpose of this site is to establish and solidify the brand and attitude of Roast. The people who will use this site are anyone looking for a nearby coffee shop or want to see the menu, or those interested in learning more about the philosophy behind Roast.

The primary goals of the users for this site are to be exposed to what Roast has to offer, get a feel for the store and the culture, and get directions to our location.

About

Established in 2016, Roast is a haven for coffee lovers. Our first batch of beans was personally picked up in Uraguay by our founder, lovingly known as ‘Uncle Jack’ in his 1969 red Ford pickup. Our commitment to curating, roasing, and brewing the best cup of coffee is the reason customers return, time and time again. We can't wait for you to make your home here at Roast.
